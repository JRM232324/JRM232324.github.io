# About Me

I am a Cyber Security and Forensic Computing graduate with a strong interest in digital forensics, malware analysis, vulnerability assessment, and incident response.

During my studies, I gained hands-on experience with tools such as Autopsy, FTK Imager, Wireshark, IDA Pro, GDB, Procmon, and PEview, alongside programming in Python and C. My academic work has strengthened my analytical thinking, technical research, and problem-solving skills, particularly through projects involving malware analysis, digital forensics, and information security.

I created this portfolio to document my projects, share technical write-ups, and demonstrate the skills I have developed as I begin my career in cyber security.
